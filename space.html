space module¶

Space module implements the abstract search space representation that is used in the optimization algorithms. Provides functionality for sampling from the search space and creating dependency relationships between parameters.

Bases: space.Space

A conditional search space is a sample space that depends on the result of other parameters (i.e. three parameters are sampled where the third must be between the two)
